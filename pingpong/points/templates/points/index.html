Rules

1. Normal matches are played to 11 points
2. Players alternate serving every two points, and players can score regardless of who serves
3. If a game is tied at 10-10 (deuce), a player must win by 2 points, and in this scenario, players alternate every serve
4. During serves, the ball must make contact with the paddle behind the table after a toss
5. If the ball makes contact with the net during a serve, it is declared a let serve and the point is replayed. There is no penalty for let serves regardless of how many times they occcur.
